The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Persuit of Happyness.

An inspiring story about a struggling salesman who overcomes tremendous odds to create a better life for himself and his son.

The Act of Racing in the Rain

An emotional story narrated by a wise dog who recounts his life with his racer-driver owner and the powerful bond they share.

The Shawshank Redemption

A gripping drama about an innocent man's journey through prison life, forming friendships and finding hope in the darkest times.
